Overview

Defining goals and outcomes

We partnered with Lumana to modernize their digital brand and streamline their product story. The result is a scalable design system, fast pages, and a refined on-brand aesthetic that translates across devices.

The experience balances clarity and motion, leading with narrative sections, full-bleed visuals, and subtle micro-interactions that guide users without overwhelming them.
